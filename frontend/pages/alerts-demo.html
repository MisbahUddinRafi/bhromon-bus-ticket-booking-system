Alert Messages Demo

Reusable alert components with earthy green design system

✓ Success Alerts

showSuccess('Success!', 'Your booking has been confirmed.');
✓
Success!
Your profile has been updated successfully. All changes are now visible.

✕ Error Alerts

showError('Error!', 'Please check your input and try again.');
✕
Error!
Email already exists in our system. Please try with a different email address.

⚠ Warning Alerts

showWarning('Warning!', 'Your reservation expires in 10 minutes.');
⚠
Warning!
Your login session will expire in 5 minutes. Please save your changes.

ℹ Info Alerts

showInfo('Info', 'Your confirmation email has been sent.');
ℹ
Information
Your ticket confirmation has been sent to your registered email. You can also view it in your dashboard.
